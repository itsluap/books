# The Meltdown of Sector Nine

A tale of pride, oversight, and responsibility in a world built from precision and friendship. When an advanced reactor in Sector Nine spirals out of control, four friends in bright hazmat suits step into a dead zone to make things right—and to make sure it never happens again.
